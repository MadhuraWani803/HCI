DELHI AGRA - 5 Days

Travel Itenary


DAY 1

Delhi – Agra (2 Nights)
Tour guests will arrive at Delhi airport as per their scheduled flight. Upon arrival they will meet Veena World tour manager at the airport and proceed to Agra – a city on the banks of the Yamuna River. Upon arrival we visit Mehtab bagh – a beautiful garden overlooking the Agra Fort and Yamuna River, provides a picture-perfect view of the Taj. In the evening we have a short Meet & Greet session

DAY 2

Agra
Today we visit Agra Fort – one of the UNECSO World Heritage Sites in India. Then we visit Taj Mahal – considered to be the epitome of the Mughal, later we get to see the Inlay work display. In the evening we enjoy boating in Yamuna River.

DAY 3

Agra – Fatehpur Sikri – Mathura – Vrindavan – Delhi (2 Nights)
Today we visit Fatehpur Sikhri and witness the glorious history of India. We then proceed to Mathura and Vrindavan to visit some famous and divine temples. We then proceed to Delhi – The capital city of India.

DAY 4

Delhi
Today we visit Raj Ghat. We also drive past India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan and Parliament House, followed by a visit to Bahai Temple – Lotus temple and Qutub Minar. We then visit Red Fort – also known as Lal Qila was built by Shah Jahan in the mid-17th century is now a UNESCO World Heritage site. Later we visit Akshardham Temple and witness the spectacular sound & light show.

DAY 5

Delhi – Departure
Today we visit Agrasen ki Baoli – a famous step well. Later we have free time for shopping. It’s time now to say goodbye to our travel companions. Let’s stay in touch with each other through email, phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and meet again on yet another memorable tour. See you all!! Tour Guests will proceed to Delhi airport as per their scheduled flight and start their return journey back home.
Tour Concludes.
