JAPAN WITH ALPINE ROUTE - 10 Days

Travel Itenary


DAY 1

ARRIVE IN TOKYO
Arrive in Tokyo. Proceed to see Asakusa Temple Complex, Nakamise Street , Renkoji Temple - where ashes of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose have been preserved since 1945 . Then visit the Tokyo Sky Tree, to experience stunning views of Tokyo from the observation deck. Overnight in Tokyo or similar.

DAY 2

TOKYO
After breakfast visit Edo Museum - a museum of the history of Tokyo during the Edo period. Later visit one of the busiest street in Tokyo Shibuya crossing. You will also get a chance to visit Bic Camera Akihabara Electronics Shop – one of the largest electronics stores. Later in the evening visit Odaiba Island where we will see Toyota Mega Web, Palette Town, Venus Fort, Rainbow Bridge At the end of the day you will be enjoying Yurikamome Train ride (Driverless Train) – Aomi to Daiba (short train ride). Overnight in Tokyo or similar.

DAY 3

HITACHI SEASIDE PARK , ASHIKAGA FLOWER PARK
After breakfast proceed to visit Hitachi Seaside park- one of the gorgeous park which home to an abundance of colorful flowers including beautiful pale blue nemophila in spring. Then we will visit Ashikaga Flower Park. The Japanese love flowers, and wisteria are among their favorites which is also called as Fuji and One of the best places to view fuji flowers is the Ashikaga Flower Park. This Park features lots of blue, white and pink fuji, as well as yellow laburnum. Overnight in Omiya or similar.

DAY 4

MT. FUJI – 05TH STATION, HAKONE ROPEWAY, LAKE ASHI CRUISE
After breakfast proceed to Mount Fuji 5th station – Japan’s tallest peak and an active volcano, to enjoy spectacular views of the mountain. Also visit Gotemba Peace Park. Proceed to take a ride on the Hakone Ropeway. The 30-minute journey from Sounzan Station to Togendai Station, which nestles on the shore of Lake Ashi, allows visitors to take in such spectacular views as the crystal-clear blue waters of Lake Ashi, the rising volcanic fumes of Owakudani and the grandeur of Mount Fuji on a fine, sunny day. Then proceed to take a cruise on the stunning Lake Ashi. Overnight in Matsumoto or similar.

DAY 5

ALPINE ROUTE
Today we proceed for Alpine route tour – which is famous for the high snow walls that line some of its roads in spring. Journey of this route starts from Tateyama Station. Enjoy amazing mountain views while travelling through Kurobe Gorge (the deepest gorge in Japan). Overnight in Kanazawa or similar.

DAY 6

TODAI JI TEMPLE, NARA DEER PARK
Proceed to Nara, to visit Todaiji Temple – A UNESCO World Heritage site is one of Japan's most famous and historically significant temples and a landmark of Nara. Visit Nara Deer Park – A home to hundred freely roaming deer. Then we move further to Osaka, on arrival we will have photo stop at Osaka Castle Overnight in Osaka or similar.

DAY 7

NIJO CASTLE, KINKAKU-JI TEMPLE, KIMONO SHOW, SAGANO BAMBOO GROOVE
Today you will visit Nijo Castle and beautiful Kinkaku-ji - one of Kyoto’s leading temples. It is also known as The Temple of the Golden Pavilion & is recognized by UNESCO as a World Cultural Heritage. You will also enjoy Kimono fashion show in Kyoto. Later proceed to Sagano Bamboo Groove, is known for its rich bamboo stalks located in the Arashiyama mountains Overnight in Osaka or similar.

DAY 8

AKASHI KAIKYO BRIDGE, MT ROKKO CABLE CAR
In the morning visit Akashi Kaikyo Bridge Exhibition Centre where we will get to know how the world’s longest suspension bridge i.e. Akashi Kaikyo Bridge was built. Then enjoy Mt Rokko Cable Car ride. At Rokko Sanjo Station at the top, there is the Tenrandai observation deck from where you can get that panoramic view of the city. Enjoy Bullet train experience from Kobe to Hiroshima. Overnight in Hiroshima or similar.

DAY 9

HIROSHIMA, MIYAJIMA
Today we will visit Bomb Dome, Sadako Monument and the amazing structure of the Memorial Cenotaph, dedicated to the ordeal of the nuclear attack that the city suffered and the memories of its victims. Later, we visit the Hiroshima History Museum and Peace Park. Then we will go to Miyajima island, to see Itsukushima Shrine, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The shrine was established in the time of Empress Suiko Overnight in Hiroshima or similar.

DAY 10

RETURN HOME
Enjoy delicious breakfast at the hotel. Check-out and proceed to the airport to board your flight back home with eternal memories of your Japan tour only with Mango Holidays.
Tour Concludes.
